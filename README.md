# 驻转拽转 - Hebrew Party Game

A fun and engaging Hebrew party game where teams compete through three exciting stages of charades-style gameplay!

 **Play Now**: [https://pitkiyot.siach.pro/](https://pitkiyot.siach.pro/)

## About the Game

**驻转拽转** (Pitkiyot) is a popular Hebrew party game perfect for groups. Teams take turns acting out words and phrases through three progressively challenging stages:

1. **专 驻砖** - Free speech (describe without saying the word)
2. ** 转** - One word only
3. **驻** - Pantomime (no words, gestures only)

### Features
-  Multiple teams support
- 憋 Timed rounds with scoring
-  Random team selection for fair play
-  Mobile-optimized responsive design
-  Audio feedback and timer sounds
-  Full Hebrew/RTL support
